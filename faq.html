Payments & Contributions
Each active member contributes KSh 50 per working day. This is recorded by the Chairperson or collector at the stage. Payments accumulate in the group welfare fund.

Welfare & Benefits
Active members in good standing are eligible for welfare support in the event of a bereavement or medical emergency. The amount is decided by the group and disbursed by the Chairperson. You must be up to date with contributions to qualify.
Your name is removed from the active members list so you no longer appear on the daily payment screen. Your past payment history is kept so the group's income totals remain accurate — the contributions you made were real money received.
Money Storage & Withdrawals
No.This app does not hold, store, or control any funds. It is a record-keeping and transparency tool only. All financial transactions and balances exist outside the app. The app simply records and displays contribution data so every member can track their own standing and the group's activity.
All group funds are deposited and held with UNAITAS SACCO. To send money to the group account, use:

Paybill: 544600
Account: 942540

Always keep your M-Pesa confirmation code after sending — you will need it to confirm your payment in the app.
No.Withdrawals cannot be initiated or processed through this app under any circumstances. The app is for records and visibility only — it has no payment or disbursement functionality.

All withdrawals from the UNAITAS SACCO account must be requested through the group's official process and can only be executed by registered signatories.
Signatories are group officials who have been formally registered with UNAITAS SACCO and are the only individuals authorised to approve and process withdrawals on behalf of the group. No single person can withdraw funds alone — the SACCO requires agreement from the registered signatories before any disbursement is made.

App & Notifications
Install the app on your phone: on Android tap the three-dot menu in Chrome → Add to Home Screen; on iPhone tap the Share button in Safari → Add to Home Screen. When prompted, tap Allow. You will then receive alerts whenever payments are recorded.
Check your internet connection. If you installed the app as a PWA, some pages may load from cache when offline. Open your browser and navigate to the site directly to force a fresh load.
