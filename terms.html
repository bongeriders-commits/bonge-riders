Section 2
Eligibility for Membership

To join, an applicant must:

  • Be an active PSV operator (bodaboda rider or matatu crew) at the Bogonko-Ngelani Stage, Katani
  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Provide accurate and truthful personal information during registration
  • Pay the one-time Stage Fee of KSh 10,000 via M-Pesa Paybill 544600, Account 942540
  • Receive approval from the Stage Chairperson

The Stage reserves the right to reject any application at its sole discretion.

Section 3
Registration & Account

Members are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate and up-to-date information and notifying the Chairperson of any changes
  • Keeping their 4-digit PIN confidential — the default PIN is the last 4 digits of your registered phone number
  • Any activity conducted through their account
  • Contacting the Chairperson immediately if they suspect unauthorised account access
Section 4
Daily Contributions
4.1 Contribution Rate

Each active member contributes KSh 50 per working day, collected physically at the stage and recorded by the Chairperson or collector in the App.

4.2 Back-Dating & Missed Days

The Chairperson may back-date payments to the correct date. Members may pay for multiple missed days at once; each day is recorded individually.

4.3 Non-Payment

Members persistently in arrears risk losing welfare eligibility and may be suspended or removed by the Chairperson.

Section 5
Welfare Benefits
5.1 Eligibility

To receive welfare support, a member must be active, in good standing, and up to date with contributions at the time of applying.

5.2 Covered Events
  • Bereavement — Death of the member or an immediate family member (spouse, child, or parent)
  • Medical Emergency — Hospitalisation or serious illness of the member

The Stage does not guarantee welfare for any other event unless resolved by a majority decision at a general meeting.

5.3 Welfare Amount

The amount disbursed is decided by the Chairperson or Stage meeting based on the available Welfare Fund balance. No fixed amount is guaranteed.

5.4 Disqualification

A member will not be eligible for welfare if they are in arrears, suspended, or their claim is found to be fraudulent.

Section 6
Stage Contribution Fee

The Stage Fee of KSh 10,000 is a one-time, non-refundable joining contribution. It must be paid via M-Pesa Paybill 544600, Account 942540. It is not refundable under any circumstances, including voluntary withdrawal, suspension, or removal.

Section 7
Member Conduct & Obligations
  • Treat fellow members and Stage officials with respect
  • Comply with decisions made at Stage meetings
  • Report suspected fraud or misuse of the Welfare Fund to the Chairperson
  • Not share your PIN with any other person
  • Not attempt to falsify or interfere with records in the App
  • Not submit fraudulent welfare applications
  • Notify the Chairperson if you leave or take an extended break from the Stage
Section 8
Suspension & Termination
8.1 Voluntary Withdrawal

A member may withdraw at any time by notifying the Chairperson. Your profile will be deactivated; past records are retained for audit purposes. No refunds are made.

8.2 Involuntary Removal

The Chairperson may suspend or remove a member for persistent non-payment, fraudulent claims, providing false information, or conduct harmful to the Stage. The member may appeal at a general meeting. The Stage majority decision is final.
